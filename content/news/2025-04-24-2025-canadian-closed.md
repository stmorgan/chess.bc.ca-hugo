Congratulations to **IM Ray Kaufman** who finished tied for 3rd in the 2025 Canadian Closed, held in Toronto on Easter weekend.  Also excellent results for **FMs Ryan Yang** and **FM Ethan Song** tied for 5th place.

**Joanne Wangluo**, BC’s representative in the 2025 Canadian Women’s Championship finished with a very respectable 4 out of 9.
