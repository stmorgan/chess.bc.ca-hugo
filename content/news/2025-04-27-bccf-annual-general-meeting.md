The BCCF Annual General Meeting will be held during the 2025 Paul Keres Memorial at Trinity Western University in Langley.  Start time will be 3pm on Sunday, May 18th .

All members are welcome to attend.  Voting age is 14, age for holding a position on the executive is 18.
