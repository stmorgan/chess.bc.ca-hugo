The Canadian Open Chess Championship is underway at the Sheraton Vancouver Guildford Hotel
15269 104 Ave, Surrey, BC. Five rounds have been played. Results and pairings can be viewed at
[chess-results.com](https://s2.chess-results.com/tnrWZ.aspx?lan=1&tno=1215798)
Information on the turnament can be found on the VanCity Chess website at [www.vancitychess.ca/tournaments/canadian-open.html](https://www.vancitychess.ca/tournaments/canadian-open.html).

Games of the top boards can be viewed live at [livechesscloud.com](https://view.livechesscloud.com/#5bddf929-2afa-4dbb-adb4-e5ff6a472010).

Times for the remaining rounds are Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday at 6:00 pm. The final round on Friday, July 18, will start at 12:00 pm.

On Tuesday, July 15, the Canadian Open Blitz Championship will be held at the site from 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM. See this [link for details](https://www.vancitychess.ca/tournaments/canadian-open-blitz-championship-2025.html).
