date: 2025-01-02
expiry_date: 2025-01-16
---

Today the CFC did a system-wide boost of ratings to compensate for rating deflation post-COVID. Players who played the most games gained the most points, the theory being that those were the players that lost the most points. For those interested there is a more detailed explanation on the CFC website under "[CFC Forums](https://www.chesscanada.info/forum/showthread.php?6586-CFC-2024-Ratings-Adjustment)”
