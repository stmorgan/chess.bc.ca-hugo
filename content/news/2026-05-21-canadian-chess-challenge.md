The annual Canadian Chess Challenge (grades 1-12) took place in Winnipeg last weekend.  Team BC tied for second with Quebec, two match points behind perennial winner Ontario who won every match.  BC lost to Ontario and Nova Scotia and defeated all the other provinces including Quebec in match play.
