The 2026 Pan American Junior Chess Championship was held in El Slavador last weekend.  Canada had 28 representatives, about one-third from BC.

Medal Winners:

U7 Female.  **Echo Fan** 9/9, 1st, **Yaswitha Suresh** 7/9 tied 2nd

U15 Female. **Joanne Wangluo** 6.5/9, 3rd

U7 **Pengyu Chen** 6.5/9, 3rd

U13. **Barath Subramanian Suresh** 7/9, tied 1st

U15. **NM Zachary Liu** 5.5/9, tied 2nd
