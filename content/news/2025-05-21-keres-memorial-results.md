Congratulations to **Neil Doknjas** and **Yidong Cheni**, co-winners of the 2025 Paul Keres Memorial with scores of 5-1.

**T.G.M. Peiris** won the U2000 Section with a perfect 6-0.

**Nico Xiao Huang** won the U1500 Section, scoring 5.5 out of 6.

**Dmytro Makogonsky** and **Jashandeep Singh** tied for top U1100 at 5-1.

205 players competed at Trinity Western University in Langley.
