**FM Ethan Song** has withdrawn from the Closed and will be replaced with **Tristan Taylor**.

This year’s BC Championship will take place 10-13 October at the Fraser Valley Chess Academy in Langley. Participants will be:

**IM Ray Kaufman** **FM John Doknjas** **FM Grigorii Morozov** **NM Neil Doknjas** **NM Yidong (Jason) Chen** **NM Keith MacKinnon** **Tristan Taylor** **Lia Rathburn** The BC Women’s Championship will be held at the same place.  Players rated 1800+ will receive free entry if entered by 30 September.

There will also be an open swiss side event.  Space is limited.
