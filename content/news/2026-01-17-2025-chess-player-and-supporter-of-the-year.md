We are pleased to announce that **Victoria Doknjas** was voted **2025 Chess Supporter of the Year** by the Executive for her hard work and dedication to bring the 2025 Canadian Open and CYCC to BC for the first time in 13 years.

We are also pleased to announce that **Karson Lu** was voted **2025 Player of the Year** for winning the under 8 age group at the CYCC, 1st place at the North American YCC and tie for 2nd at the World Cadet.  Karson gained over 700 rating points in 2025.
