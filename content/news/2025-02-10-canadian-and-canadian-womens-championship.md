For those interested, the **2025 Canadian Championship and Canadian Women’s Championship** will be held in Toronto 17-22 April.  The highest finishing player from each of the 2024 BC Championship and BC Women’s Championship to attend the tournament in Toronto will receive a $1,000 travel grant from the BCCF.
