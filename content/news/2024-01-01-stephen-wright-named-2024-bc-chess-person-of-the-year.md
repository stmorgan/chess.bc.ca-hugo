title: "Stephen Wright Named 2024 BC Chess Person of the Year"
date: 2024-01-01
expiry_date: 2024-01-15
---

I am pleased to announce that your executive has chosen **Stephen Wright** as 2024 BC Chess Person of the Year.  He is our bulletin editor, a chess coach, chess historian and international arbiter.  He was cited for efficient work and professional support to the chess community.  I would like to add that as Past President he has been an invaluable resource for me to call on throughout the year.

- Paul Leblanc, BCCF President
