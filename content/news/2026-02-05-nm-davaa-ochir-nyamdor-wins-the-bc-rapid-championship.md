Congratulations to **NM Davaa-Ochir Nyamdor** for winning the BC Rapid Championship last Sunday. Davaa finished with 6 points out of 7 in an extremely strong 110 player event that included 3 IMs, 3 FMs and 6 NMs. Also thank you to Vancouver Chess School and IA Stephen Wright for putting on this year's championship.
