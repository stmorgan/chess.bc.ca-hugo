Congratulations to the 2024 BC Junior Champion, **Ryan Jingyun Yang**.

Ryan won the title last weekend on tie-break with **Justin Xu**.  Both finished with 4.5-0.5 after drawing in their individual encounter in round 4.

In addition to the travel grants awarded by the event, the BCCF will provide $1,000 for travel expenses to the top finisher who attends the 2025 Canadian Junior Championship.

The Reserve Section was won by **Jesse Law** with a perfect 5-0 score.

The Booster Section was won on tie-break by **Josiah Lo** (5-0) over **Rockwell Chan** (also 5-0).

Thank you to all the organizers, arbiters and parents who made this event possible.
