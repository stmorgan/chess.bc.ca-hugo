Thank you to **Umang Banatwala** and the rest of the Fraser Valley Chess team for a really great Keres on Victoria Day weekend. There were 197 players in the Open and 39 in the Blitz.

Winners of the sections were:

Premier: **GM Vladimir Belous** and **FM/WIM Megen Lee**, both of Seattle tied for first with 5 points out of 6.  **FM Grigorii Morozov** as the top BC player (on tie-break) qualified for an invitation to the BC Closed.

U2000: **Gyuwan Lee** 5.5 points

U1500: **Tianyou Huang** 5.5 points

U1000: **Weihan Chen** and **Lucas Zhou** 5 points

Blitz: **GM Vladimir Bilous** and **NM Yidong Chen** 6 points out of 7
