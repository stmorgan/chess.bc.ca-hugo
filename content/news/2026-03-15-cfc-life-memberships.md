Tired of being pestered to renew your CFC membership?  Consider a life membership.  It pays for itself after a few years, you lock in today’s membership prices and you never have to worry about renewing again.

The proceeds from life membership sales are invested in the Chess Foundation of Canada which invests the funds on behalf of the CFC and provides an annual stream of income for the CFC.

[https://www.chess.ca/en/players/membership-fees/](https://www.chess.ca/en/players/membership-fees/)
