Congratulations to 2025 Canadian Open co-winners **FM Ryan Yang** (Vancouver), **GM Shawn Rodrique-Lemieux** (Montreal), **FM Tymur Keleberda** (Edmonton) and **FM Stephen Willy** (Washington State).  All scored 7/9.

U 2000 winner was **Aiden Hao-Ting Lu** (Vancouver) with 8/9.

U 1600 winner was **Zachary Halfar** (Burnaby) with 7.5/9.

246 players participated.
