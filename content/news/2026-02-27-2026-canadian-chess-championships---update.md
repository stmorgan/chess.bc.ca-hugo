The dates and locations for the Canadian Chess Championships have been announced.

-
Canadian Closed and Zonal 2-7 April in Montreal

-
Canadian Women’s Closed and Zonal 2-7 April in Montreal

-
Canadian Junior  Championship 27-30 June in Calgary

-
Canadian Senior Championship 27-30 June in Calgary

**NM Neil Doknjas**, winner of the 2025 BC Closed will receive the BCCF travel grant to the Canadian Closed.

**Dahlia Quinn**, 2nd place on tie-break at the 2025 BC Women’s Championship will receive the BCCF travel grant to the Canadian Women’s Championship. Update: **Dahlia Quinn** will not be attending.  **Joanne Wanglou** will be BC’s representative.

**NM Kate Jiang**, winner of the 2025 BC Junior Championship has been invited to receive the BCCF travel grant and represent BC at the Canadian Junior.  If she declines, the invitation will go to the next highest finisher willing to attend.

The winner of the 2026 BC Senior Championship, to be held in Surrey 5-7 June will be invited to receive the BCCF travel grant and represent BC at the Canadian Senior.  If the winner declines, the invitation will go to the next highest finisher willing to attend.

Details for the national championships can be accessed through the CFC website [Events](https://www.chess.ca/en/events/) page.
