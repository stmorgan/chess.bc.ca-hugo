If you would like to add your club to the list, or would like to update your
information, please contact the webmaster (email address is on the listing of
the [Executive](/executive/)).

Chess clubs come and go and you may find some of the listings out of date.
To help judge the likelihood of being current, we list the date of the last
contact we had with the club. If you are a club organizer, it would be helpful
if you contacted us occasionally to confirm the club information listed is
still correct.
