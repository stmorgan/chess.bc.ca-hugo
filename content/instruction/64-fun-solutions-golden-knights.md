Our activity promotes the study and knowledge of the game of chess, for its
own sake as a sport, art and science, but also as a means for the improvement
of our multicultural society. Our aim is to have chess recognized as a sport
in Canada and make it an important tool in educating and developing life long
logical thinking, social and organizational skills of the young generation.
