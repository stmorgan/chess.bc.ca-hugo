At FVCA, we make learning and fun come together to create a long lasting
impression on our students and their families. We not only just teach our
students how to play Chess – we preach how to grow beyond the game! FVCA's
unique curriculum nurtures problem solving skills, builds confidence to handle
pressure situations with a calm mind and uncovers the hidden potential of the
human brain. We are currently running group lessons at Langley Downtown and
private home lessons in Surrey, Coquitlam and Maple Ridge. Ages 6+.
