Chess isn't just a game - it teaches us how to make better life decisions.
Membership offers casual tournament play as well as in-person lessons. If
you've always wanted to learn chess, or improve your skills, it's time to make
your move! Lessons are taught by a BC-certified teacher with over 15 years of
experience. Dejan designed a chess course, which is taught at Terry Fox
Secondary School for credit. He is passionate about his love of the game and
teaching in the community that he calls home.
