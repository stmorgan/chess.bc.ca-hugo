---
title: "Life Members"
---

The BCCF no longer issues new life memberships. Existing life memberships are
honored by giving an exemption to BCCF tournament fees. Please note, BCCF life
membership is not the same as CFC life membership. Many members have CFC life
membership but not BCCF life membership.
